How to Export Google Calendar Events to CSV (2025)

Published: April 2025 | Updated: April 2025

Exporting your Google Calendar events to CSV is useful for backups, data analysis, or migrating to another calendar service. This guide walks you through the exact steps.

Step 1 — Open Google Calendar Settings

Go to calendar.google.com and click the gear icon in the top right corner. Select Settings from the dropdown menu.

Step 2 — Navigate to Import and Export

In the left sidebar, scroll down until you see the Import & Export option. Click it to expand the export options.

Step 3 — Export Your Calendar

Click the Export button. Google will generate and download a ZIP file containing your calendar in ICS format. To convert this to CSV format, use a free online ICS-to-CSV converter tool.

Why Export Google Calendar to CSV?

CSV files are widely supported by spreadsheet tools like Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets. Exporting your calendar data lets you analyze events, create reports, or migrate to a different service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Google Calendar export directly to CSV?

No. Google Calendar exports in ICS format. You need to convert the ICS file to CSV using a third-party tool.

Is this method safe?

Yes. You are downloading your own data directly from Google. No third-party access to your account is required.
